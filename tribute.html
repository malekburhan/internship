Tribute to Shah Rukh Khan

Introduction

Shah Rukh Khan, also known as the "King of Bollywood," is an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality born on November 2, 1965.

Personal Life

Shah Rukh Khan was born in New Delhi, India. He is married to Gauri Khan, and the couple has three children - Aryan, Suhana, and AbRam.

Career in Bollywood

Shah Rukh Khan has appeared in more than 80 Bollywood films and earned numerous awards for his performances. His versatility and charisma have made him one of the most successful and beloved actors in the Indian film industry.

Global Icon

With a massive fan following not only in India but also around the world, Shah Rukh Khan has become a global icon. His contributions to Indian cinema have earned him recognition and accolades internationally.

Philanthropy and Business Ventures

Beyond acting, Shah Rukh Khan is involved in philanthropy and owns production companies. He is known for his charitable work and support for various social causes.

Awards and Achievements

Shah Rukh Khan has received numerous awards for his contributions to the film industry, including multiple Filmfare Awards and the Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian award.
